Token Model
TEMCO tokens and incentive points are awarded as a token of appreciation for your participation in the community. To maintain a sustainable system, monthly top reviewers, highly ranked businesses, and community-voted feedback writers are rewarded. You can spend the tokens in the TEMCO marketplace for your next purchase.
